# Billing and Licensing

The Billing and Licensing domain provides observability into Relativity's licensing and billing processes, ensuring accurate data transmission and compliance with licensing requirements. This domain focuses on monitoring the health and activity of agents responsible for billing and telemetry operations, which are critical for maintaining uninterrupted access to your Relativity environment.
